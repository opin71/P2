About Me: Janette Nelson

My diverse experience of 25 years has led to a multi-career path which comprises of short-term and long-term contract positions in both the private and public sector.

I have worked in the pioneering stages of global digital entertainment communities and market intelligence subsequently, I have witnessed their evolution e.g. Napster to Spotify (peer to peer vs client server architecture - centralised and decentralised storage), the analogue to the digital revolution, as well as the transference from business intelligence to cultural asset mapping and data centricity

This could be why digital transformation is a fluid concept for generation X (which includes me) because we are at the relevant axis of practical awareness and experience, which provides us with the ability to translate between analogue and digital fluidly.

I have worked as a Retail Product Trainer and Tactical Technical Brand Representative for Smart Technologies brands and these are some of the companies I have worked for e.g Sennheiser (Smart Headphones and Speakers), Bowers and Wilkins (Smart Headphones and Speakers) Hive (Smart Home), in gaming for ACER Predator (PC and Laptop) and in telecommunications for Three Mobile (Go Roam).

The latter has driven my passion for tech and the influence of strategy, diversity, innovation as well as the impact of value on disruptive cultures, technologies and markets.

Competencies

I have diverse transferable skills and experience combining advanced digital art direction and production, advanced project management, intermediate front-end web development, advanced digital video and audio streaming production and intermediate back-end CPanel management.

I have intermediate HTML, CSS, SQL and Javascript experience and I have just began to learn Python.

In addition to this I have GDPR, Data Protection and data sharing legal contracts, regulation, notices and policy experience.
