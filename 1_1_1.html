Unit 1, Outcome 1.1

Describe the purpose of IT in modern business

To use the below examples from my own global and national business development experience to answer this question I have listed who I have worked for and what I have taken away from these companies in terms of an understanding of the purpose of IT in modern business

Exigent Group: a global legal digital services, software and outsourcing consultancy.

Adaytum Software: Internet-based enterprise business planning software ( I worked with the founder and CEO Guy Haddeleton of Adaytum when it was not internet based software! when it was a start-up in a small team in Australia (CEO, CIO and 2 x Business Development Managers in Australia and he sold Adaytum to Cognos)

Proudfoot: Global Management Consultancy

Taylor Nelson Sofres: One of the largest reseach companies worldwide.

Comscore (former Jupiter Media Metrix): A media measurement and analytics company providing marketing data and analytics to enterprises; media and advertising agencies; and publishers.

Sensis Australia:Sensis Australia: Digital platforms and Marketing Company

In reference to the above companies and from my experience of selling their IT products and services I would say IT is used for enterprise resource planning and management: data management systems, customer relationship management, inventory management systems, financial management systems, information management systems, legal management systems, B2B Research, marketing/advertising/social media management and communications between employers and other business colleagues.
